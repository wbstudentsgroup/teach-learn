# T&L (Teach & Learn)

T&L (Teach & Learn) is a platform that connects students with qualified tutors across West Bengal. Students can find tutors for home tuition, online classes, or learning at the tutor's place. Tutors—including college students, graduates, and experienced teachers—can register to offer their teaching services and connect with learners in their local area.

## Features

* 📚 Find verified tutors
* 🎓 Become a tutor and earn by teaching
* 🏠 Home tuition, online, and tutor-place options
* 📍 Location-based tutor matching
* 🤝 Partnership with WB Students Group
* 📱 Mobile-friendly and responsive design

## Technology

* HTML5
* CSS3
* Google Forms (Registration)
* Google Sheets (Data Collection)

## Project Goal

Our mission is to make quality education accessible while creating flexible earning opportunities for tutors across West Bengal.

## Website

Students can:

* Find a tutor
* Submit tuition requirements

Tutors can:

* Register as a tutor
* Share their teaching profile
* Receive student leads
